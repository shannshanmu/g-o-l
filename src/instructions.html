• this is a simulation of conway's game of life
  • click on a tile to place a green tile that can move with neighbours
  • make sure your tile has at least 2/3 neighbours to live
  • any less than 2 neighbours and any more than 3 neighbours means tile will disappear
  • try to make an infinite game of life!
  • n.b. cheat keys included, find out what they are!!1!
  • clear the board (c) often before clicking random keys to find cheats
  • p to pause life processes in game so nothing dies, r for randomly generated set of tiles
  • up arrow to increase speed, down arrow to decrease speed.
  • for more info, check wikipedia:
  • https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conway%27s_Game_of_Life
